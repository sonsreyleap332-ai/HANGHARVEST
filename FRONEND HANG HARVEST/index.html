WELCOME TO HANG HARVEST AGRICULTURE

Hang Harvest Agriculture Co.,Ltd, located in Trapeang Prasat District, Oddar Meanchey Province, around 30 kms away from Choam Sangam international port. We have been long experienced in tapioca production and trading since 2010. Our tapioca chips reputation is obviously well known as one of the quality major tapioca source of the north part of Cambodia supplying to local animal feed factory, and one of the big tapioca export to Thailand. With rapid growth of our agriculture products, now announced to penetrate tapioca chips export market to China. Currently Hang Harvest Agriculture export tapioca chips regularly to Thailand, Vietnam, and China. With the support of the Royal Government, now we have loading port can serve both for loading in containers and break bulk shipment. We are also expanding our tapioca chips plant and loading center in Phnom Penh city where located around the main river for logistic future of our export products which is leading to be the one in top range of tapioca chips export of Cambodia.

DRIED TAPIOCA CHIPS

Cambodia is the largest tapioca chip producer export to Thailand and Vietnam which is mostly for ethanol production. We have produced dried tapioca chip and export to Thailand for 13years, and become one of the top Cambodia tapioca exporters. Now, we have extended our dried tapioca chips market to China directly.

TAPIOCA CHIPS

Tapioca chips are used widely in China, Brazil as well as Korea in the making of alcohol as a substitute to molasses and sweet potato. They are processed from raw tapioca roots into dry chip form. In making chips, the roots are chopped into small pieces and sundried on concrete floor for 4 to 5 days until the 65% moisture content is reduced to about 16% to 18% by weight. The chipping process is mainly designed to reduce the size of the roots, to shorten drying time and to make transport easier after drying.

Specification of products
